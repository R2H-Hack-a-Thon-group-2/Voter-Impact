NC Polling Information

Polls are where the votes take place and it's important that you know the details that surrond them.There are two main types of ways to vote: absentee or mail-in, and in person. Polls are your in person option and have a set number of diverse locations to fulfill your need and time. Early voting is often done at polls, so if you are considering voting early the first step would be to find a poll location near you. In addition to early voting, voting on Election Day (November 3rd, 2020) will take place at a poll.
